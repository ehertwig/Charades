Charades

Rules: Split players into two balanced teams. Younger players may pick from the Level 1 category. Have one of the teams call "Heads" or "Tails" to determine who goes first. Flip the coin! Each actor will have 90 seconds to act out the image or word on the card. If the team guesses correctly, advance the team score by one. First team to reach 10 points, wins! Each team alternates actors after each turn. No noises, mouthing words, or writing words in the air. Also, no clapping, slapping, hitting, kicking, or crying...Freddy. Play on, Players!
